Increase in complaint ID's regarding student loans from Apr '21 to May '23 in California

By Sofia Appolonio

The state of California saw a 3 million user increase in complaint ID's from April 2021 to May 2023 for Federal Student Loans, according to data provided by The Navient Coorporation

The data below displays a sharp increase in the ammount of complaint IDs regarding Federal Student Loan servicing, with many user's claiming that the issue with their loan related to poor information on the loan from Navient or issues with how the payments were handled.
